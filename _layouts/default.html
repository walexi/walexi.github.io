Research

I'm generally interested in building learning algorithms; drawing inspiration from the intersection of statistical physics, computational neuroscience and computer science (broadly speaking - system 2 learning). I'm also interested in geometric deep learning (many real-world data-sets can naturally be framed as graphs)

On the application side, I'm interested in recommendation systems, drug discovery and humanitarian applications of AI such as fighting racism.
